Instruments

Woodwinds
  • Saxophone
    • : Has a reed and can be played in alto or soprano, is gold and has 23 keys
  • Clarinet
    • : Has a reed and can be played in alto or soprano, is black and has 17 keys
  • Oboe
    • Is double reeded and can be played in alto or soprano, it is black and has 45 keys
Brass
  • Trumpet
    • : is gold, has a mouthpiece, played in tenor, is gold and has 3 valves
  • Trombone
    • : is gold, has a mouthpiece, played in tenor and has a slide pull and push
  • Tuba
    • : is gold, has a mouthpiece, played in tenor, and has 6 valves
Percussion
  • Snare
    • : a single drum, can be multicolored, is to be struck to create sound
  • Bells
    • : a series of metals, usually is silver, is to be lighlty struck to create sound
    • Triangle: a single piece of metal, usually is silver, is to be lighlty struck to create sound
